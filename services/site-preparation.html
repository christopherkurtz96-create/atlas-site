Site Preparation Services

Complete site clearing and preparation for new construction projects.

Professional Site Preparation in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides comprehensive site preparation services that set the foundation for successful construction projects. From raw land to build-ready sites, we handle every aspect of preparing your property for new construction.

Our Site Preparation Services Include:

  • Vegetation Removal - Clearing trees, brush, and undergrowth
  • Debris Removal - Removing existing structures, concrete, and debris
  • Rough Grading - Initial grading to establish proper site contours
  • Topsoil Stripping - Removing and stockpiling topsoil for later use
  • Cut and Fill - Moving earth to create level building pads
  • Erosion Control - Installing silt fencing and erosion measures
  • Access Roads - Creating temporary or permanent access routes

Residential & Commercial Site Prep

Whether you're building a new home, developing a subdivision, or preparing a commercial property, we have the equipment and expertise to prepare your site properly. Good site preparation is essential for successful construction outcomes.
