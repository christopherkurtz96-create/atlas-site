Concrete Removal Services

Driveways, patios, foundations, and commercial concrete demolition.

Professional Concrete Removal in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides efficient concrete removal services for residential and commercial properties throughout Columbia and the Mid-Missouri region. Whether you need to replace an old driveway, remove a damaged patio, or demolish commercial concrete structures, we have the equipment to handle your project.

Our Concrete Removal Services Include:

  • Driveway Removal - Complete driveway demolition and removal
  • Patio Removal - Breaking up and hauling away concrete patios
  • Sidewalk Removal - Removing damaged or unwanted sidewalks
  • Foundation Removal - Demolishing old foundations
  • Slab Removal - Breaking up and removing concrete slabs
  • Pool Deck Removal - Removing concrete around pools
  • Commercial Concrete - Parking lots, floors, and structures
  • Debris Hauling - Complete removal to recycling facilities

Why Remove Old Concrete?

  • Replace cracked or damaged surfaces
  • Prepare for new construction or landscaping
  • Improve property appearance and value
  • Eliminate trip hazards
  • Create better drainage solutions
  • Expand or reconfigure outdoor spaces
