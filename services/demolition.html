Demolition Services

Mobile homes, houses, garages, concrete, and commercial structures cleared safely and completely.

Professional Demolition Services in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides safe, efficient demolition services for residential and commercial properties throughout Columbia and the Mid-Missouri region. From mobile home removal to complete building teardown, we handle demolition projects of all sizes with professionalism and care.

Our experienced team follows all safety protocols and local regulations to ensure your demolition project is completed properly, with all debris removed and the site left clean and ready for your next project.

Our Demolition Services Include:

  • Mobile Home Removal - Complete mobile home demolition including skirting, decks, steps, and foundation cleanup
  • House Demolition - Full residential structure demolition with proper permitting and disposal
  • Garage Demolition - Attached and detached garage removal
  • Barn & Outbuilding Removal - Agricultural structures, sheds, and outbuildings
  • Concrete Removal - Driveways, patios, sidewalks, foundations, and slabs
  • Commercial Demolition - Office buildings, retail structures, and industrial facilities
  • Interior Demolition - Selective interior tearout for renovations
  • Pool Removal - In-ground and above-ground pool demolition

Our Demolition Process

We take a systematic approach to every demolition project to ensure safety and efficiency:

  • Site Assessment - We evaluate the structure, identify hazards, and plan the safest approach
  • Permitting - We handle all necessary permits and utility disconnections
  • Hazard Removal - Proper handling of any hazardous materials identified
  • Demolition - Controlled demolition using appropriate equipment
  • Debris Removal - Complete removal and proper disposal of all materials
  • Site Cleanup - Final grading and cleanup leaving your site ready for the next phase

Why Choose Atlas for Demolition?

Demolition requires experience, proper equipment, and attention to safety. Our team brings all three to every project:

  • Fully licensed and insured for your protection
  • Experienced operators with proper training
  • Modern equipment maintained to high standards
  • Proper disposal at licensed facilities
  • Clean, thorough site cleanup included
