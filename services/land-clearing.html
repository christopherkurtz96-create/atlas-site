Land Clearing Services

Tree removal, brush clearing, and vegetation management for your property.

Professional Land Clearing in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides comprehensive land clearing services for property owners throughout Columbia and the Mid-Missouri region. Whether you're preparing land for construction, creating pasture, or improving your property, we have the equipment to clear your land efficiently.

Our Land Clearing Services Include:

  • Tree Removal - Clearing trees of all sizes from your property
  • Brush Clearing - Removing brush, shrubs, and undergrowth
  • Stump Removal - Grinding or removing stumps below grade
  • Root Removal - Clearing root systems for construction
  • Lot Clearing - Complete clearing for building lots
  • Fence Row Clearing - Clearing overgrown fence lines
  • Right-of-Way Clearing - Clearing for roads and utilities
  • Pasture Reclamation - Reclaiming overgrown pasture land

Why Clear Your Land?

Land clearing is often the first step in many property improvement projects:

  • Preparing building sites for new construction
  • Creating usable outdoor spaces
  • Improving property aesthetics and value
  • Reducing fire hazards
  • Expanding agricultural use
  • Installing fencing or outbuildings
