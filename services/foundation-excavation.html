Foundation Excavation Services

Accurate foundation digs for residential and commercial buildings.

Professional Foundation Excavation in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides precise foundation excavation services for new construction throughout Columbia and the Mid-Missouri region. A properly excavated foundation is critical for the structural integrity of your building, and our experienced team delivers accuracy on every project.

Our Foundation Excavation Services Include:

  • Basement Excavation - Full basement digs with proper depth and dimensions
  • Crawl Space Excavation - Shallow excavation for crawl space foundations
  • Slab Preparation - Grading and preparation for slab-on-grade foundations
  • Footing Trenches - Precise trenching for continuous and spread footings
  • Pier Holes - Excavation for pier and beam foundations
  • Commercial Foundations - Large-scale foundation excavation
  • Addition Foundations - Excavation for home additions
  • Backfill - Proper backfilling after foundation work

Why Precision Matters

Foundation excavation requires careful attention to detail. Excavating too deep or too shallow, or failing to maintain proper dimensions, can cause serious problems for your construction project. Our team uses modern equipment and careful measurement to ensure your foundation excavation meets exact specifications.
