Mobile Home Removal Services

Complete mobile home demolition and removal with site cleanup.

Professional Mobile Home Removal in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition specializes in mobile home removal throughout Columbia and the Mid-Missouri region. Whether you're clearing land for new construction, removing a damaged trailer, or cleaning up an abandoned property, we provide complete mobile home demolition and removal services.

Our Mobile Home Removal Services Include:

  • Complete Mobile Home Demolition - Breaking down the entire structure
  • Skirting Removal - Removing all skirting materials
  • Deck & Porch Removal - Demolishing attached decks and porches
  • Steps & Ramp Removal - Removing access structures
  • Foundation Cleanup - Removing blocks, piers, and runners
  • Debris Hauling - Complete removal of all materials
  • Site Cleanup - Leaving your property clean and level
  • Optional Site Prep - Grading for new construction

Why Remove an Old Mobile Home?

  • Prepare land for new home construction
  • Clear property for sale
  • Remove storm-damaged or condemned structures
  • Clean up inherited or abandoned property
  • Eliminate eyesores and liability concerns
  • Improve property value

The Removal Process

We handle the entire process from start to finish:

  1. Site assessment and estimate
  2. Utility disconnection coordination
  3. Demolition and breakdown
  4. Material sorting and loading
  5. Hauling to proper disposal facilities
  6. Site cleanup and grading
