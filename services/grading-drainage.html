Grading & Drainage Services

Precise grading for proper drainage and water management solutions.

Professional Grading & Drainage in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides expert grading and drainage services to protect your property from water damage and create level, usable outdoor spaces. Proper drainage is essential for protecting foundations, preventing erosion, and maintaining healthy landscapes.

Our Grading & Drainage Services Include:

  • Yard Grading - Leveling and sloping yards for proper drainage
  • French Drains - Underground drainage systems to redirect water
  • Swales - Shallow channels to direct surface water flow
  • Finish Grading - Final grading for landscaping and sod
  • Driveway Grading - Grading for new driveways and parking areas
  • Foundation Drainage - Protecting foundations from water intrusion
  • Erosion Control - Preventing soil erosion on slopes
  • Retention/Detention Areas - Water management basins

Signs You Need Drainage Work

  • Standing water in your yard after rain
  • Water pooling near your foundation
  • Soggy or muddy areas that won't dry
  • Erosion channels forming in your yard
  • Basement or crawl space moisture issues
  • Downspout water not draining away from home
