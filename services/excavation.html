Excavation Services

Site prep, grading, land clearing, foundation digs, and drainage work for your next project.

Professional Excavation Services in Mid-Missouri

Atlas Excavation & Demolition provides comprehensive excavation services for residential and commercial projects throughout Columbia and the Mid-Missouri region. Our experienced team uses modern equipment to deliver precise, efficient results on every job.

Whether you're preparing a site for new construction, need land cleared for development, or require precise grading for drainage, we have the expertise and equipment to get the job done right.

Our Excavation Services Include:

  • Site Preparation - Complete clearing and preparation of construction sites, including debris removal and rough grading
  • Land Clearing - Tree removal, brush clearing, stump grinding, and vegetation management
  • Grading & Leveling - Precise grading for proper drainage, yard leveling, and site preparation
  • Foundation Excavation - Accurate foundation digs for basements, crawl spaces, and slab foundations
  • Trenching - Utility trenching for water, sewer, electric, gas, and fiber optic lines
  • Drainage Solutions - French drains, swales, and grading for water management
  • Pond & Lake Excavation - Digging and shaping for ponds and water features
  • Driveway Excavation - Preparation and grading for new driveways

Why Choose Atlas for Excavation?

We understand that excavation work sets the foundation for everything that follows. That's why we take a careful, methodical approach to every project. Our team:

  • Uses GPS-guided equipment for precise grading
  • Follows all local regulations and permitting requirements
  • Maintains clean, organized job sites
  • Communicates clearly throughout the project
  • Stands behind our work with satisfaction guarantee

Residential & Commercial Excavation

From small residential projects to large commercial developments, we scale our services to meet your needs. Our fleet of equipment includes excavators, skid steers, dozers, and dump trucks to handle projects of any size.

We work with homeowners, builders, contractors, and developers throughout Columbia, Ashland, Harrisburg, Hallsville, Boonville, Fulton, Centralia, Rocheport, and the surrounding Mid-Missouri area.
